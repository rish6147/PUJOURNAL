There are around XXXX research scholars and XXXX Post Graduate students who contribute largely towards the Research publications from the Institution. An average of XX to XX research scholars with the institution complete their doctoral degrees every year.

As part of the technical activities of the college and also to publish the outcomes of the research activities carried out in the campus, the IJFCET was initiated as an in-house journal in the year XXXX. Thanks to the persistent efforts made by the Institution, the journal received its ISSN code in the year XXXX making it a refereed journal. Henceforth, the in-house journal transformed into a national journal so as to increase its distribution as well as to include contributions from other academic and research institutions. The journal was made a quarterly journal from XXXX onwards by publishing four issues per year in the months of March, June, September and December.
